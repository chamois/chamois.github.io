TREC On-Ramp Using LEGO EV3

TREC On-Ramp

Students build and program robots to complete sensor-assisted navigation tasks. Students are also provided with the opportunity to explore physical sensor functionality and attachment, engineering labs, and STEMScience Technology Engineering and Math. STEM is a way to understand the world around you. It will help to empower you to succeed in many different fields. explorations.

Foundational Concepts:Science and Engineering Practices, Computational Thinking, Mathematical Processes and Proficiencies, Physical Sensor Functionality, Programming, Program Flow.
